- HexIsles -

February 21st 2021

Help the hexes reach their goal without leaving anyone behind! A little puzzle game about the hexagons life in exile.

This game was made in 7 days for Brackeys Game Jam 2021.1 to the theme "Stronger Together" in Unity 3D.
